How we work

Process · v2.1.0
We start with what you want, but we confirm with labs and a physician review before any prescription. The $79 Wellness Assessment is our front door for hormones, weight loss, and peptides — IV Lounge is walk-in without a consult.

Lane A — IV Lounge (walk-in)

  1. Patient walks in or books IV Lounge online.
  2. IV screening edge function runs hard blocks (CHF, pregnancy, G6PD for high-dose vitamin C, etc.).
  3. Select drip or push; charge at checkout (signature drips $139–$169; NAD+ $50 booster; glutathione and other pushes).
  4. Members receive 20% off IV add-ons beyond the 2 included drips on ELEVATED IV.
  5. Soft mention $79 Wellness Assessment only if hormone/weight interest surfaces.

Lane B — Consult-gated (Rx · peptides · hormones)

  1. Patient pays $79 Wellness Assessment via Stripe before or at booking.
  2. In-person visit: history, vitals, goal confirmation.
  3. Order goal-appropriate lab panel; draw same visit when possible.
  4. Run CDS assessment in provider chart (staff) → physician review (prescriber only).
  5. Results call: enroll program, collect consents, schedule RN teaching.
  6. Never promise a specific medication before labs and physician sign-off.

What one care bundle includes (never duplicate on add-on)

  • Monthly RN check-in (both protocols reviewed in one visit)
  • Unlimited secure messaging
  • Quarterly in-office labs (Expanded panel when GLP-1 is in the mix)
  • Physician review and protocol adjustments when clinically indicated

Enrollment process (Provider Dashboard)

  1. $79 Wellness Assessment + baseline labs ($199 hormones · $299 if GLP-1 in mix)
  2. Provider review — document all lanes: weight, hormones, recovery, vitality, metabolic adjuncts
  3. Pick anchor = primary clinical goal (weight → GLP-1 anchor; hormones → TRT/HRT anchor)
  4. If second Rx lane indicated → select medication add-on in Elevated Combo Selector (one subscription, two line items)
  5. Send combo checkout link OR update existing subscription via update-subscription-addon
  6. Peptides (layer 3): after consent + gates → separate Send Payment Link per SKU (member −20% each line)
  7. Quote total monthly = combo total + sum of peptide lines (show math on paper)
  8. Chart: elevated_program (anchor) + elevated_program_addon + peptide SKUs in care plan
  9. Staging OK: physician may start anchor only; add medication add-on after 8–12 weeks stable

Always

  • Stack additional discounts on top of member 20% on peptides
  • Say plus pharmacy costs on TRT/HRT/GLP-1 anchor or add-on lines
  • Skip Expanded labs when GLP-1 is in the combo — one draw covers both lanes

Margin guardrails

  • Medication add-ons priced at full program minus $100 — preserves ~30%+ gross margin on drug COGS

  • Recovery stack ($349) is the only published peptide bundle SKU — saves vs $498 à la carte BPC+TB

Rules · v2.1.0

Policy reminders

  • Medication is INCLUDED in ELEVATED TRT / HRT / GLP-1 — never say “plus pharmacy costs.”
  • NAD+ is ONLY the $50 IV booster push — no peptide NAD+, no standalone NAD+ infusion.
  • Ketamine / Spravato — not offered (legacy).
  • Retatrutide — physician-selected within GLP-1 lane only; full consent; never headline or advertise.
  • Injectable TRT (cypionate) — not offered. Anastrozole and HCG — not offered.
  • Cash pay · no insurance billing · itemized receipts & superbills on request.
  • Benefits end on membership cancel: quarterly labs revert to full price; 20% discount ends.

Do say

  • "Our ELEVATED programs bundle your medication, RN check-ins, quarterly labs, and messaging — one price."
  • "Every new patient starts with a $79 Wellness Assessment unless they're IV walk-in only."
  • "Active members get 20% off à la carte — IV, peptides, extra labs."
  • "We are cash-pay; we can provide itemized receipts and superbills on request."
  • "IV Lounge is walk-in — no consult required for drips and pushes."

Never say

  • "Plus pharmacy costs" or "medication billed separately" on TRT/HRT/GLP-1.
  • Promise a specific drug or dose before labs + physician review.
  • Offer ketamine, Spravato, injectable TRT, anastrozole, HCG, or pellets.
  • Advertise or lead with retatrutide — physician-gated only.
  • Standalone NAD+ infusion or peptide NAD+ — NAD+ is the $50 IV booster only.
